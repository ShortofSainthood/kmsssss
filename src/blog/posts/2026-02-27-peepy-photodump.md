[ItemLabel](https://itemlabel.com/) is a company that makes, among other things, weird collectable art toys. In 2021 they became semi-viral for their YouTube ads, which features music from the company's CEO and CXO [emamouse](https://emamouse.bandcamp.com/). Their flagship character is Peepy (pronounced like "pee-pee"), a furby-like creature who likes to commit crimes. They also make OuiOuis (pronounced like "wee-wee"), which are bipedal, baby versions of peepys, and a bunch of other characters.

<!-- excerpt start -->
I started collecting itemLabel plushies last year. I wanted to include some recent photos of them in my next monthly recap post, but I realized that I had so many stashed away in my phone that I should probably share the old ones first. So This post will just be a dump of those, along with my assorted thoughts.

For a while it felt like my craving for wacky looking plushies had been satiated, but then I heard the news of itemLabels then-newest line of blind bags.

<figure class="small right">
	<img src="/assets/images/allstars_back.png" alt="A chart showing all the different plushi designs available in OuiOui Allstars.">
</figure>

Typically, I'm not a huge fan of blind bags. They're a real gamble, half of the time you end up getting something that you don't even want. ItemLabel's products aren't exactly the cheapest, so I really don't want to spend money on them unless I really like what I'm getting. Not to mention these are *art* toys that I'm just going to display on my shelf anyway. I'd need a *really* good reason to actually purchase a blind bag.

However, when Itemlabel announced OuiOui Allstars, every single one of the designs was an *absolute banger*!

The line features the best designs from older blind bag serries that they don't/rarely restock anymore, as voted by the fans in their discord server. Perfect for someone like me who only started collecting recently. They also come with cute little accessories! So I guess if you're a hardcore OuiOui collector that already has all of the originals, you can try to collect all of these to get all of the accessories instead.

In November 2025 I ordered myself some OuiOuis and words can not describe how excited I was when I opened it.

I got **Strawberry**{ .pink } and **Stinker**{ .purple }! Like I said, I'd be satisfied with getting any of the designs, but I do find the little strawberry outfit to be especially cute. I also like that one of them is smiling and the other isn't. I didn't bother giving these guys proper names either, so if you have any suggestions feel free to let me know!

If I could made one small critique; the clothes are a little hard to get on and off the plushies. I kept worrying that I was accidentally going to damage them or my OuiOui, because you really need to force it on them. It took me several minutes just to get Stinkers pants on without them falling off. usually this wouldn't bother me, but since swapping the clothes between them is a highly advertised feature on itemLabel's website/social media, I thought it was worth mentioning.
