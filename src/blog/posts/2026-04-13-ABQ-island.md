**⚠️ This post may contain minor spoilers for Breaking Bad and Better Call Saul.** (Yes, really.)

I've been so excited for the full release of Tomodachi Life: Living the Dream! Unfortunately, while I was playing the demo I bought an item from the clothing shop, which effectively ends the demo and keeps all mii events from happening. I can't do anything until the full game actually releases and I can transfer my save data.

So, to quench my extreme thirst for Tomodachi Life content, I booted up what I thought was a fresh save for the old Tomodachi Life on 3DS, only to realize that I already started a save file for the game about a year ago.

<!-- excerpt -->

I barely remember doing it, but apparently I made this save file Breaking Bad/Better Call Saul-themed. I named my island ABQ island (short for Albuquerque), made my "look-alike" Walter, and filled it up with characters from Breaking Bad.

To be honest, playing this way isn't as fun as just having a massive crossover of whatever characters you want, or just making up your own characters, but whatever. I just wanted to play the game and didn't want to bother with all the tutorials and unlocking all the areas, which were mostly unlocked on this file, plus I'll probably never touch this again after the new game comes out, so it all works out for me.

Here are some screenshots from my playthrough so far. These probably won't be that funny to you unless you're a fellow fan of either show though, so your mileage may vary.
